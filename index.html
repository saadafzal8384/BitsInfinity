ABOUT US

BitsInfinity is a high-tech enterprise dedicated to industrial data management. The business covers multiple industries and is committed to the construction of the big data industry ecological chain. We adopt advanced technology to realize the full life cycle management of data from collection, processing to application. Our products are applied to various fields such as the Internet, finance, government, and intelligent manufacturing.
