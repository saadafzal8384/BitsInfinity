ABOUT US

BitsInfinity is an industrial data management company. We provide large numbers of stable services to manage data that generated from different fields of industry.

BitsInfinity is a high-tech enterprise dedicated to industrial data management. The business covers multiple industries and is committed to the construction of the big data industry ecological chain. We adopt advanced technology to realize the full life cycle management of data from collection, processing to application. Our products are applied to various fields such as the Internet, finance, government, and intelligent manufacturing.

VISION & VALUES

We envision becoming a leading digital service provider in the domain of Industrial data management. We strive to create value for our customers by enabling them to make smarter, in-time, and cost-effective decisions by using our digital services which are driven by industrial standards to manage industrial data intelligently and effectively.
